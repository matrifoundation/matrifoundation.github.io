About Us

Matri Foundation is an initiative to eradicate violence by imparting moral education to children and providing 24/7 psychological care.

Fact : 85% of people involved in violence related crimes have been a victim of very unfavourable growing condition.

Lack of moral education and drastic cultural differences takes the major blame for brutal rapes and murder happening in India.

We, volunteers at Matri Foundation aims at providing best psychological care for every children
