About Us

Matri Foundation is an initiative to eradicate violence by imparting moral education in children and providing 24/7 psychological guidance.

85% of people involved in violent crimes have been a victims of a disturbed childhood themselves.

Although most of the criminals are 18 or older, the seed is planted at a very young age. Thus, lack of moral education and exposure to cultural, social or sexual intolerance in childhood creates an adult with a disturbed mind capable of the most heinous crimes known to humanity.

A child if aided at the right time can not only himself diverge from the wrong path but also guide others near him.

We, the volunteers at Matri Foundation aim to provide the best possible psychological guidance for every child in need.
